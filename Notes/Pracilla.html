About Us

At Fundacion Adelante Guatemala, we confront the acute challenge of hunger that plagues many regions of our nation. The poignant stories of families in the rural highlands, confronting the specter of starvation daily, fuel our commitment. Our resolve hardened when we witnessed children, the very embodiment of our future, languishing from lack of basic nutrition.

This narrative, unfortunately, is common in the verdant landscapes of Guatemala, where beneath the beauty lies a hidden struggle. Our organization was conceived amidst this struggle, established to offer hope and practical solutions to those battling food insecurity.

It was in the year 2001 when our founder, Luisa Moreno, driven by a profound encounter with a starving child in the streets of Antigua, vowed to make a difference. This powerful moment led to the birth of Fundacion Adelante Guatemala. What began as a small initiative to distribute meals in one community has grown into a nationwide effort to foster self-sufficiency and improve health outcomes.

Our foundation's story is one of unity and resilience, propelled by the belief that no one should face the day uncertain of where their next meal will come from. We are driven by the vision of a Guatemala where every individual has the nourishment they need to thrive and the opportunity to lead a life of dignity.

We invite you to be part of our journey as we continue to advocate for the hungry, the disenfranchised, and the hopeful. Together, we can build a legacy of prosperity and nurture the seeds of change in Guatemala.
